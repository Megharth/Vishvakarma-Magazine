About Vishvakarma

Vishvakarma-the personification of creation. The name itself refers to someone, who is the icon of his/her creation. Vishvakarma, where not only the talent and the art gets recognized, but also the adroitness for your Work.

We, at Vishvakarma provide you the platform to not only one myriad other art Forms. From the Field of Literature, to the field of Artistry, Photography, and Documentation we are always eager to receive your inputs.

Who are we? Some of the professional teachers? Or some Paid Employees? No, we are a team of Students who are hunters of talent and who will enthusiastically encourage creativity and flair. We aspire to help you fly with the wings of your Dreams.

To help you push forward, every year we publish a Magazine "Vishvakarma-The College Magazine" which helps you to express your own thoughts, emotions, art, poetry to other people. To connect with like-minded crowd and to create magic out of Morphemes

Wake up from your dreams, it’s time to achieve them! Vishvakarma is waiting for you.

As I am told that the annual edition of the Vishvakarma magazine is getting published, it is immense pride that engulfs me. As torchbearers of Charutar Vidya Mandal’s motto for educating of young minds, the penultimate goal has always been to help learners unravel their way to the successes they deserve, to attain the knowledge that they seek and serve the world with their innovative outlook. And now seeing them emerge gracefully from their cocoons of sole academic excellence to exploring the creative facets in life along with it , the metamorphosis indeed gladdens my heart.

Thus for the very same reason, I’d like to take this esteemed opportunity to convey my heartfelt gratitude to Dr. CL Patel sir, Hon. Chairman, CVM, BVM BOG and all the secretaries of CVM for all their helpful guidance and throughout the year. It is owing to their support that we are successfully completing two years of BVM’s autonomous status.

Leafing through these pages has always been a joy. I wish everyone in the team “Vishvakarma 2015”-all the faculties, administrative-technical staff, chief editors, convener Dr. Namita Sathe and participants and everyone involved a very hearty congratulations for continuing the legacy.

And last but not the least, we thank you dear reader, and the students of BVM for your interest and support to the magazine.

May God keep BVM eternally blessed.
